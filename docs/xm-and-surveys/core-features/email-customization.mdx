Email branding is a white-label feature that allows you to customize the email that is sent to your users. You can upload a logo of your company and use it in the email.

<Note>Email branding is part of the Formbricks [Enterprise Edition](/self-hosting/advanced/license).</Note>
<Note>Only the Owner and Managers of the organization can modify the logo.</Note>

## How to Upload a Logo

1. Go to the Organization Settings page.
2. You will see a card called **Email Customization** under the **General** section.

![Email Customization Settings](/images/xm-and-surveys/core-features/email-customization/email-customization-card.webp)

3. Upload a logo of your company. Logos must be 5 MB or less.
4. Click on the **Save** button.

![Updated Logo](/images/xm-and-surveys/core-features/email-customization/updated-logo.webp)

## Viewing the Logo in the Email

You can click on the **Send test email** button to get a test email with the logo.

![Email Sample](/images/xm-and-surveys/core-features/email-customization/email-sample.webp)

## Use Cases

- **White-Labeling**: Customize all outgoing emails with your organization logo and branding to remove default Formbricks branding from respondent-facing communications.
- **Branding**: Add your company logo to every email sent by Formbricks, reinforcing brand recognition with survey respondents and team members.
- **Survey Distribution**: Send branded link surveys directly to contacts via email, ensuring recipients see your organization identity throughout the survey experience.
- **Notification Emails**: Response completion notifications sent to your team carry your organization branding for a professional, cohesive look.
- **Invitation Emails**: Brand member invitation emails with your organization logo so new team members recognize your organization immediately.
- **Follow-Up Emails**: Automated post-response follow-up emails include your branding for a consistent experience.

<Note>Follow-up emails are part of the Formbricks [Enterprise Edition](/self-hosting/advanced/license). An active enterprise license is required to use email follow-ups.</Note>

## Legal Footer Configuration

All transactional emails sent by Formbricks include a legal footer section for regulatory compliance. The email template supports configurable legal properties that self-hosted instances should set to comply with email regulations such as CAN-SPAM and GDPR.

The email template wrapper accepts the following legal configuration properties:

| Property | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| `privacyUrl` | URL to your organization's privacy policy page |
| `imprintUrl` | URL to your organization's imprint or legal notice page |
| `imprintAddress` | Physical mailing address displayed in the email footer |

The footer is rendered by `packages/email/src/components/email-footer.tsx` and the legal links are rendered within the shared template at `packages/email/src/components/email-template.tsx`. Footer text is translatable via the internationalization system using translation keys such as `emails.email_footer_text_1` and `emails.email_footer_text_2`.

<Note>
  **Compliance Requirement**: If you are self-hosting Formbricks, ensure your email footer includes a valid physical mailing address and links to your privacy policy and imprint pages. These are required by email regulations including CAN-SPAM (United States) and GDPR (European Union).
</Note>

## Email Template Components

Formbricks uses a set of shared React Email components to ensure consistent styling and branding across all transactional emails. All email types use the shared `EmailTemplate` wrapper for a unified layout.

| Component | File | Purpose |
|-----------|------|---------|
| Email Template | `email-template.tsx` | Shared wrapper that renders the branded layout including logo, content area, and legal footer for every email |
| Email Button | `email-button.tsx` | Call-to-action button component used in emails for actions such as verifying an email or resetting a password |
| Email Element Header | `email-element-header.tsx` | Section header component for rendering question headlines and subheaders in embedded survey emails |
| Email Footer | `email-footer.tsx` | Footer component that renders translatable legal compliance text at the bottom of every email |

Source: `packages/email/src/components/`

When a custom organization logo is uploaded, the `EmailTemplate` wrapper replaces the default Formbricks logo with your organization's logo across all email types. The template also renders imprint and privacy policy links in the footer section when configured.
